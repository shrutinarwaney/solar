Kinetic Energy Calculation of Hydropower

  • Pmax =½ηρv^2
  • p=density=1m3
  • n=efficiency of turbine=0.85
  • v=velocity=1m/s

Heat Energy Calculation of Solar Energy

  • E = Energy (kWh)
  • A = Area (m2:196m^2)
  • r = efficiency(%: 0.2)
  • H = Solar radiation(6.8 kwh)
  • PR = Performance ratio(0.5-0.9 or there is the default value = 0.75)


  • E = A * r * H * PR
  • E = 196m^2*0.2*6.8 kwh/m2/day*0.75
  • E = 196.2888 kWh
