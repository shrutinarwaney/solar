The solar panels are very environmentally safe because, not only do they not harm any living ecosystems like wind power do and there is no chance of earthquakes like there is with some geothermal ways of getting energy, they are also leed certified, which means that they are very energy-efficient. Problems

A problem that could rise regarding the solar panels is that as the factory continues to expand and supplies more workers with more computers and other technology, more solar panels may need to be implemented. If more and more people join the factory in the long term, this could potentially increase the cost drastically as well as take up space needed for the factory.
