Space

A small hydropower system would be in a stream or a river, so it would not interfere with human activity very much and does not take up much space, and, because the small hydropower systems are so small, it is very rare that the salmon would get caught. The hydropower systems will also include certain prevention systems such as salmon cannons and other fish cannons as well as other camera technology to prevent smaller fish from getting stuck within it. The systems will also be developed with protective material and will most certainly be eco friendly. The approximate amount of space is about 40 meters, but it ranges depending on how high the current is and where you are.

Cost

The small hydropower will cost about $1,300 for 8,000 kw of energy, which is the same as 8 megawatts, and $500 to install, resulting in $1,800. The only thing is that, every four years, the price goes up 1%.

Enviornmental Impact

The small hydropower method is mainly environmentally safe, as it does not use any fossil fuels or non-renewable energy resources, but there have been reports of salmon dying, but that is very rare, and this usually happens in regular hydropower systems. There are also prevention systems that can keep the fish from dying.
